# 06 - Product Brief

## Product Name

GreenNest BuildFlow

## One-line Description

GreenNest BuildFlow is a project operating system for housing development and construction investment teams to manage legal readiness, documents, tasks, decisions, construction progress, finance and executive reporting in one place.

## Problem

Housing development projects usually run across fragmented tools: chat, spreadsheets, email, folders, paper records and informal meetings. This creates several recurring problems:

- Founder cannot see project health quickly.
- Legal and document readiness is unclear.
- Tasks and decisions are not consistently assigned or followed up.
- Important files are hard to find or versioned inconsistently.
- Delays, missing documents and blockers are discovered late.
- Reports take manual effort and often depend on scattered information.

## Target Customers

Primary:

- Small to mid-size real estate developers.
- Housing development companies.
- Construction investment teams.
- Founder-led project organizations.

Secondary:

- Legal/project consulting teams.
- Construction management firms.
- Design and development management teams.

## Primary Users

- Founder/Owner.
- CEO/Project Director.
- Project Manager.
- Legal Manager.
- Assistant/Document Controller.
- Engineer/Design Manager.
- Construction Manager.
- Finance/Accounting Manager.

## Core Value Proposition

GreenNest BuildFlow gives project leaders one trusted operating view across project status, legal readiness, missing documents, overdue tasks, blocked decisions and execution risk.

## Product Pillars

1. Project operating core.
2. Legal and planning readiness.
3. Document control.
4. Task and accountability management.
5. Executive dashboard and reporting.
6. Construction and quality execution.
7. Finance and commercial control.
8. AI-assisted project intelligence.

## MVP Promise

In MVP V1, users can create projects, manage tasks, track documents, update legal checklist status and view a dashboard generated from structured project data.

## Long-term Promise

The product becomes the control center for the full project lifecycle: investment, legal, design, construction, finance, reporting and decision support.

## Competitive Positioning

GreenNest BuildFlow is not a generic task manager and not a full ERP at the beginning. It is a domain-specific operating platform for housing development projects, starting with project/legal/document visibility and expanding into execution and finance.

## Success Signals

- Founder checks dashboard weekly or daily.
- PMs update tasks and blockers inside the system.
- Legal team updates checklist and related documents.
- Reports are generated from data instead of manually assembled.
- Missing documents and overdue tasks become visible before meetings.
