# 00 - Product Vision

## 1. Vision

GreenNest BuildFlow is a project operating system for housing development and construction investment companies.

The product should help founders, owners, project managers and specialist teams control the full lifecycle of a real estate development project: from land review and legal preparation to design, construction, handover, finance, reporting and post-project learning.

The long-term goal is not just task management. The goal is a structured operating platform where every project has one source of truth, every risk has an owner, every document has a status, every decision is traceable and executives can understand the health of the business without chasing fragmented chats or spreadsheets.

## 2. Strategic Product Outcomes

- One central workspace for every project.
- Clear legal and planning readiness tracking.
- Document control with version, owner, status and related workflow.
- Task and responsibility management across teams.
- Construction progress, quality, contractor and material tracking.
- Executive dashboard for risks, blocked items, missing files, schedule and budget.
- Meeting decisions converted into accountable action items.
- Financial visibility for budget, cost, commitment, payment and cash flow.
- Audit trail for important operational changes.
- AI assistance for risk detection, missing document suggestions, priority recommendations and report drafting.

## 4. Product Principles

- Project is the central container.
- Data must be entered once and reused everywhere.
- Statuses, roles, document types and workflow steps must be configurable over time.
- Every critical item needs an owner, deadline, status and history.
- Dashboard must be generated from real data, not manually typed reports.
- MVP can be simple, but architecture must not block later enterprise capabilities.
- Vietnamese is the first UI language; internal keys should support future localization.
- Mobile should support quick review and update, while desktop remains the primary operating surface.

## 6. Non-goals For Early Phases

- Native mobile apps.
- Full ERP replacement.
- Full BIM/CAD editing.
- Government portal integration.
- Accounting system replacement.
- Deep procurement marketplace.
- AI making final legal or financial decisions.

## 7. Success Metrics

Operational:

- Projects created and actively updated.
- Percentage of tasks with owner and due date.
- Percentage of required documents with status.
- Reduction in unknown/missing legal steps.
- Weekly report generation time.

Executive:

- Number of risks detected before escalation.
- Late tasks by project.
- Blocked legal steps by project.
- Missing critical documents.
- Budget variance visibility.

Adoption:

- Weekly active users.
- Number of updates per project per week.
- Percentage of meetings with action items.
- Repeat usage by founder/PM/assistant.
